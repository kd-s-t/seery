### 2) YZi Labs Preferred Projects

#### AI-Assisted Oracles
UMA's Optimistic Oracle (OO), which Polymarket depends on, is decentralized and transparent but slow (24‚Äì48h resolution). **Opportunity**: domain-specific or AI-assisted oracles for faster, contextual resolution. (CZ also mentions prediction market oracles as an opportunity area)

#### Dispute Detection & Incentives
UMA OO can be vulnerable in low-liquidity, low-attention markets. **Opportunity**: build cross-protocol incentive layers to reward honest disputes, or autonomous dispute-bot networks to detect manipulation.

#### Better UX / Account Abstraction
Prediction markets still feel like DeFi dApps: complex wallets, bridging, and gas. **Opportunity**: leverage account abstraction and gasless UX to make prediction markets feel like normal apps.

#### Subjective Predictions
Polymarket covers only well-defined, publicly verifiable events. **Opportunity**: solve for subjective or multi-stage predictions

#### Liquidity Aggregation
Liquidity is spread across many small markets. **Opportunity**: aggregators, routing layers, or AMM-style liquidity pools to concentrate and reuse capital efficiently.
